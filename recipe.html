Chocolate Chunk Blondies

This is a simple, delicious recipe for Chocolate Chunk Brownies courtesy of Ina Garten and the Food Network.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound (2 sticks) unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 cup light brown sugar, lightly packed
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 extra-large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
  • 1 1/4 pounds semisweet chocolate chunks

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour an 8x12x2 inch baking pan.
  2. In the bowl of an electric mixer fitte with the paddle attachment, cream butter, brown and granulated sugar on the high speed for about 3 minutes (until mix becomes light and fluffy).
  3. Then, turn the mixer on low and add the vanilla extract. Then add eggs one at a time.
  4. In a small bowl sift together the flour, baking soda and salt. With the mixer on low, gently add the flour mixture to the butter mixture. Fold the walnuts and chocolate chunks in with a rubber spatula.
  5. Spread the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake exactly for 30 minutes.
  6. Cool the pan and cut into bars.

Total time: 45 minutes. Prep time: 15 minutes. Cook time: 30 minutes.

Yields: 12 blondies

Serve warm with a glass of milk.
